ROS 2 (Robot Operating System) is the industry standard for building robotic applications. In this guide, I'll walk you through the essential steps to get your first ROS 2 workspace up and running.

## Why ROS 2?

Unlike ROS 1, ROS 2 is built on DDS (Data Distribution Service), making it suitable for distributed systems, real-time control, and production environments.
