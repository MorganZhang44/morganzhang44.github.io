Frequently Asked Questions

2 min · Morgan

General#

What is your research focus?#

My research sits at the intersection of robotics and artificial intelligence. I focus on autonomous navigation, deep learning for perception, and human-robot interaction. The goal is to build intelligent systems that operate reliably in real-world, unstructured environments.

Are you open to collaborations?#

Absolutely! I’m always interested in interdisciplinary collaborations, especially those involving autonomous systems, computer vision, or NLP. Please reach out via email with a brief description of your project or idea.

Projects#

Can I use your open-source code?#

Yes — all projects listed on my Projects page with a GitHub link are open-source under the license specified in each repository. Please cite appropriately if you use it in academic work.

Do you accept contributions to your projects?#

Contributions are welcome! Check the CONTRIBUTING.md file in the relevant repository for guidelines on submitting issues and pull requests.

Academic & Professional#

Are you available for talks or guest lectures?#

I’m happy to give talks on topics related to my research. Please contact me with the event details, topic, and timeline.

Do you offer mentorship or advising?#

I mentor students and early-career researchers on an informal basis. If you’re interested, send me a short message about your background and what you’re hoping to work on.

Where can I find your publications?#

You can find my publications on Google Scholar or on my university profile page.

Contact#

What is the best way to reach you?#

Email is the fastest: you@example.com. I typically respond within 1–2 business days.

Are you on social media?#

You can find me on GitHub and LinkedIn. I don’t actively use other social platforms.
